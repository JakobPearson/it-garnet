Playground

Located within the scenic Pinnacle Mountain State Park near Little Rock, Arkansas, the playground offers a safe and enjoyable space for children to play and explore. With colorful equipment set amidst the natural beauty of the park, kids can climb, slide, and swing to their hearts' content. Parents can relax on nearby seating while keeping an eye on their children, making the playground a perfect destination for family fun in the great outdoors.

Rock Climbing

Experience the thrill of rock climbing amidst the stunning landscapes of Pinnacle Mountain State Park near Little Rock, Arkansas. With expert guidance and diverse climbing opportunities, climbers of all levels can tackle granite cliffs while enjoying panoramic views of the surrounding wilderness. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced climber, Pinnacle Mountain offers an unforgettable outdoor adventure filled with challenge and natural beauty.

Biking Trails

Pinnacle Mountain State Park offers an exhilarating network of biking trails, winding through diverse landscapes and offering stunning views of the surrounding wilderness. From beginner-friendly paths to challenging singletracks, cyclists of all skill levels can find adventure amidst the park's natural beauty.
